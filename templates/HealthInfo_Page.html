Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How can I safely dispose of expired medications?

    Contact your local pharmacy for safe disposal options, or check for local drug take-back programs.

  • What should I do if I miss a dose of my medication?

    Consult your pharmacist or healthcare provider for instructions based on the type of medication.

  • Are over-the-counter drugs safe to combine with my prescription medications?

    Always check with your doctor or pharmacist before combining medications to avoid adverse interactions.

  • How can I manage side effects from my medication?

    If you're experiencing side effects, contact your healthcare provider for advice on how to manage or adjust your treatment.
